This is Your Company Website

You can integrate an AI chatbot into your website by adding two script tags to any page. This will display a circular button in the bottom-right corner, allowing visitors to initiate a chat session.

The chatbot operates using content you define—such as your company’s terms and conditions, FAQs, service agreements, and other reference materials. This ensures responses are specific to your business context.

At the end of each interaction, a transcript of the conversation is automatically emailed to you for review or follow-up.

Add the following Script Tags to your website

    <script>
    var xfbotConfig = {
    apiEndpoint: 'https://xf.com.mt/xfai/bot/',
    userId: 'botdemo' // IMPORTANT: Update with your real USER ID,

    //Other optional configuration
    headerTitle: 'Support Bot',
    headerBgColor: '#ffd042',
    headerTextColor: '#ffffff',

    toggleButtonBgColor: '#ffd800',
    toggleButtonOpacity: 0.9,

    chatBubbleUserColor: '#333333',
    chatBubbleBotColor: '#ffffff',
    chatTextColorUser: '#ffffff',
    chatTextColorBot: '#333333',

    inputPlaceholder: 'Type your message...',
    };
    </script>
    <script src="https://psanilp.github.io/xfbot/xfbot.min.js" defer></script>
