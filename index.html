This is Your Company Website

You can add an AI chatbot to your website by inserting two script tags to any web page.

A circular button in the bottom-right corner will appear and it will allow your customers to interact with your website using AI. It will respond to information that you define! At the end of the interaction, the transcript will be emailed to you. 


<script>
    var xfbotConfig = {
        apiEndpoint: 'https://xf.com.mt/xfai/bot/',
        userId: 'botdemo',

        headerTitle: 'Support Bot',
        headerBgColor: '#ffd042',
        headerTextColor: '#ffffff',

        toggleButtonBgColor: '#ffd800',
        toggleButtonOpacity: 0.9,

        chatBubbleUserColor: '#333333',
        chatBubbleBotColor: '#ffffff',
        chatTextColorUser: '#ffffff',
        chatTextColorBot: '#333333',

        inputPlaceholder: 'Type your message...',
    };
</script>
<script src="https://psanilp.github.io/xfbot/xfbot.min.js" defer></script>
